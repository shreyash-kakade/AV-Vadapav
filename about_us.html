Ankush Dhanave

Founder & Owner of AV Vadapav

Meet Our Founder

AV Vadapav was founded with a passion for delivering authentic flavors and high-quality street food. Our owner, Ankush Dhanave has dedicated years to perfecting the art of making delicious Vadapav that everyone loves.

About Us

Established in 2008, AV Vadapav started as a small venture with a big dream—to serve the most authentic and flavourful vadapav to food lovers. Founded by Ankush Dhanave, our journey began as a small stall, driven by a passion for quality ingredients and traditional taste. Over the years, our dedication to taste and consistency has earned us a place among the top 5 famous vadapav brands in the sangli district. Today, AV Vadapav is a favourite destination for those who crave the perfect blend of spice, crunch, and flavour in every bite. Join us and experience why we are one of the most loved vadapav brands in the region!

Our Vision

"To be the most loved and recognized vadapav brand in the Western Maharashtra, known for our authentic flavors, quality ingredients, and commitment to customer satisfaction. We strive to expand our presence while preserving the rich tradition and taste that make our vadapav special."

Our Mission

"At AV Vadapav, our mission is to serve the finest, freshest, and most delicious vadapav, made with authentic spices and premium ingredients. We are committed to providing a delightful street food experience with fast service, hygienic preparation, and an unwavering passion for taste. Our goal is to bring people together over the love of food while continuously innovating and expanding our reach."
